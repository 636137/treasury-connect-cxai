# TreasuryDirect - Series I Savings Bonds

## Purchase Limits

- **Electronic I Bonds**: $10,000 per calendar year per Social Security Number
- **Paper I Bonds**: $5,000 per calendar year (only available via tax refund)
- **Total Maximum**: $15,000 per year ($10,000 electronic + $5,000 paper)

## Interest Rate Calculation

Series I Bonds earn interest through a composite rate that combines:

1. **Fixed Rate**: Set at purchase, remains for 30-year life of bond
2. **Inflation Rate**: Adjusted every 6 months based on CPI-U

**Composite Rate Formula**: 
Composite rate = [Fixed rate + (2 x Inflation rate) + (Fixed rate x Inflation rate)]

## Redemption Rules

- **Minimum holding period**: 12 months (cannot redeem before 1 year)
- **Early redemption penalty**: If redeemed before 5 years, you forfeit the last 3 months of interest
- **Tax deferral**: Interest is tax-deferred until redemption or 30 years
- **Education exclusion**: May be tax-free if used for qualified education expenses

## How to Purchase

1. Create a TreasuryDirect account at www.treasurydirect.gov
2. Link your bank account
3. Purchase bonds in any amount from $25 to $10,000 (to the penny)
4. Bonds are held electronically in your account

Reference: 31 CFR Part 359 - Series I Savings Bonds
